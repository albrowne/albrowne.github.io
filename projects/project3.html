Design Project Overview

This photograph shows the payload fairing assembly being constructed atop Ground Support Equipment (GSE), seen as the white structure on the ground. While I was not the lead designer of the GSE pictured, I developed a closely related configuration for a different application. My design maintained strict structural integrity requirements while integrating features that streamlined Launch Operations processes, reducing installation time and improving overall efficiency.
